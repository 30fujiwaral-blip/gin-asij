Spirit Day

Make YOUR Mustang Statement

What is Spirit Day? Spirit Day is an event hosted by ASIJ to show our Mustang spirit and pride, and to strengthen our community. Many events such as live concerts, international food booths, and impact booths are hosted school-wide.

GIN @ Spirit Day: We hosted a clothing drive and a fun recycling game to spread awareness about fast fashion and recycling. Our booth educated the Mustang body about the benefits of recycling and the effect trash has on the environment.

"Eco Fashion is a MUSTANG PASSION": We planned a clothing swap that collected clothes before, during, and after Spirit Day. The 2024 Winter Clothing Swap was extremely successful, showing our passion for 2nd hand clothing within the middle school.

Winterfest

ASIJ's Largest Community Celebration

What is Winterfest? In early December, the PTA's Winterfest sees over 3,000 members of our community celebrate the festive season with food, activities, shopping, and performances. Last year was ASIJ's 50th anniversary of having Winterfest!

GIN @ Winterfest: We successfully hosted an animal adoption event, bringing dogs and hamsters to the school's largest annual event. This promoted responsible pet adoption and raised awareness about animals in shelters.
