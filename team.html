Our Team

Meet the changemakers who make GIN possible

Faculty Advisor

Ms. Dee Willey

Started GIN@ASIJ, Head Teacher Organizer

Ms. Willey is the founding advisor of Global Issues Network (GIN) in ASIJ. She came to ASIJ in 2023 and has since then worked on empowering student leaders through GIN. A certified Level 2 educator in Compass Sustainability Education, Ms. Willey has years of experience working with High School GIN in the American School of Dubai where she worked on multiple projects and conferences that promoted students' advocacies anchored on the SDGs.

Ms. Willey loves working with young changemakers who care about making a positive impact to their local and global community. At her former school, Ms. Willey started the annual tradition of Falcon Souq, a student-led business fair organized by the International Business class where all profits would support the students' cause. At ASIJ, she teaches Grade 8 Humanities and Social Entrepreneurship Explo. Starting in 2025, she will also be advising MS Model United Nations.
