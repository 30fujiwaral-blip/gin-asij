JOIN GIN!

What is GIN?

Our Mission

We empower young people to collaborate locally, regionally, and globally as changemakers who build inclusive project-based sustainable solutions that address our shared global issues.

Our Vision

An empowered global Network led by young changemakers who nurture and mobilize transgenerational communities of local-global citizens to build an inclusive, equitable and sustainable world.

GIN is a place where we can act on what we are passionate about and create change. We are always open to new members who are passionate and dedicated to making a local-regional-global impact.

What Can YOU Do as a GIN Member?

Lead a Drive

Sneaker Drive Example: Students partnered with Sneaker Kids Tokyo and collected 379 pairs of sneakers in 2025, all delivered to orphanages in Cebu, Philippines. Over 1.8 million orphans in the Philippines need support.

Your idea could help even more children!

Organize Fundraisers

Bake Sale Example: Students raised ¥200,000+ over 2 years to support refugees in the Middle East. The 2025 bake sale helped Shahd, a refugee studying to become an interior designer in Turkey.

All 6 houses participated with nearly 20 bakers!

Create Art & Awareness

Earth Day Mural Example: Students collected P.E.T. bottle caps and created a recycled GIN mural on plywood, showcased at Earth Day alongside sustainability quiz games.

Turn recycled materials into powerful messages!

Host Events

Animal Adoption Example: Students partnered with ARK (Animal Refugee Kansai) and brought dogs and hamsters to Winterfest (3,000+ attendees) to promote adoption. Over 6 million dogs enter shelters annually worldwide.

Create experiences that change lives!

Combat Fast Fashion

Clothing Drive Example: "Eco Fashion is a MUSTang passion!" Students collected dozens of boxes of clothing, promoting 2nd hand clothing and running successful clothing swaps.

The fashion industry produces 10% of global carbon emissions - you can make a difference!
